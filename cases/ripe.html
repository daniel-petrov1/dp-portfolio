Adaptive design

Ripe is an investment platform focused on tokenized music royalties. The goal was to build a modern corporate website that clearly explains a complex product and reinforces trust through clean structure, motion, and precise execution.

Design system

A Webflow-based design system built to keep the project consistent and easy to scale. It includes tokens for colors, typography, font sizes, border radius, and spacing, along with open reusable components. Animations are implemented using a combination of Webflow Interactions and GSAP.
